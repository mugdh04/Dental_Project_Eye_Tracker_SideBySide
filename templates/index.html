Instructions

  1. Ensure pre and post treatment images are placed in the images/pre and images/post folders with matching filenames (e.g., 1.png, 2.png).

  3. Complete the pre-flight check to verify camera and images.
  4. Follow the calibration prompts by looking at each green dot.

  6. Press Ctrl+Q to exit early at any time.
  7. Results will be saved as CSV files in the logs folder.
